What Is Leading Wealth?

Leading Wealth is an online Multi Level Marketing Platform organized by well meaning team of concerned persons to help thousands of people have Financial Breakthrough. The platform is based on Networking Principles (multi-level marketing strategy) where every member joins his/her Referee's (Leader's) Downline and help empower him/her to Financial Freedom. The poeple in his/her downline are powered by people in their individual downlines too. The strategy is to empower someone (Downline Leader) and you in turn will be empowered by others (Downline Members).

How Does It Work?

When you register an account, you will be automatically inserted into your Referee's Downline if he/she don't have up to three users already. Else, you will be inserted into a dynamically selected Downline which is still free. At this point, you will be issued your first invoice which you must have to pay to be able to upgrade to the next level. The whole invoicing process happens automatically at each level. The amount of each invoice too is determined by the level you are.
The first time, you pay $20.00 and you get paid $20.00 * 3 users which is $60.00. The second time you pay $50.00 and get paid $50.00 * 3 users which amounts to $150.00. Next, you pay $150.00 and get paid $150.00 * 3 users amounting to $450.00 and finally, you pay $250.00 and get paid same $250.00 * 3 users which is $750.00. Do the final calculation and you will understand how much you have credited into your account by embracing Leading Wealth Networking opportunity.
